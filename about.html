About Jenga

Jenga Construction Limited is a dynamic, progressive and visionary company that offers general contracting and construction management services to residential, commercial, industrial and institutional customers in Tanzania.

The company is registered with the Contractors Registration Board as a Class VI Building Contractor. Given the Company’s steer record in the construction of different types of properties and utilities backed by a good stock of tools and equipment, efforts are now at hand to have it upgraded to Class IV. Originally, the company was conceived by four directors, each holding qualifications in some construction-based profession. There has since been a change of hands in the ownership of the Company’s shares so that the number of directors has now been reduced to three to make the company linear and easier to manage. The policy at Jenga Construction is that employees should be empowered to own some shares in the company, so that today, Jenga Construction is an employee-owned company. This creates a sense of teamwork and ownership as well as a high degree of royalty and dedication, thus ensuring quality construction and efficient management of resources.In the few years of its existence, Jenga Construction has earned a reputation for being creative, technologically advanced and extremely responsive to customer demands. Our competitive advantage stems from the atmosphere of mutual trust and respect that permeates our company. Jenga Construction is specialized in new building construction, renovation, restoration, rehabilitation, refurbishment and external works such as pavements construction and rehabilitation as well as construction of drainage system and sewages.
